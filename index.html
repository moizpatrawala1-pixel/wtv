WE'VE HEARD EVERYTHING

Do I actually have to stop drinking beer?
+
Absolutely not. Beer is in the name for a reason. Our program is designed specifically for people who drink beer. We work with your lifestyle, not against it. We'll calibrate your plan around your intake.
How quickly will I see results?
+
Most clients notice visible arm changes within 6–8 weeks. By month 3, you'll be fielding comments from everyone around you. Exact timeline depends on your starting point and consistency.
Can I join from outside India?
+
Yes — we have clients in over 30 countries. Our online coaching program is fully remote and built for global clients. Timezone? Not an issue. We make it work.

The Bicep Beast plan includes 6 months of daily coaching, a fully custom training plan, beer-compatible nutrition guide, bi-weekly video calls with Shahyan, access to our VIP community, and lifetime access to all program materials. It's everything you need.

A gym helps, but it's not mandatory. We can programme around home setups, hotel gyms, or outdoor training. We work with what you have and make it count.
